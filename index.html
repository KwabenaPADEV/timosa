Our Strategic Pillars

Pillars of the Foundation

Our work is guided by five strategic pillars that drive sustainable development, environmental stewardship, and community transformation. Through these pillars, we deliver impactful programs that improve lives, protect natural ecosystems, and empower communities to build a resilient and prosperous future.

Environmental Protection & Conservation

We are committed to preserving the environment through sustainable conservation initiatives that protect ecosystems, restore degraded landscapes, and promote responsible natural resource management.

  • Afforestation and Reforestation
  • Waste Management and Recycling
  • Biodiversity Conservation
  • Pollution Prevention

Climate Change & Resilience

We strengthen the capacity of communities to adapt to climate change by promoting climate-smart solutions, renewable energy, and resilience-building programs that reduce environmental risks.

  • Climate Change Education
  • Climate Adaptation Projects
  • Community Resilience Programs
  • Renewable Energy Promotion

Social Development

We invest in people by supporting education, health, gender equality, and youth development to create inclusive communities where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.

  • Youth Empowerment
  • Women's Empowerment
  • Education Support Programs
  • Public Health Promotion

Research & Advocacy

We promote evidence-based solutions by conducting research, influencing policy, and raising awareness to encourage sustainable environmental practices and informed decision-making.

  • Environmental Research
  • Policy Advocacy
  • Community Awareness Campaigns
  • Stakeholder Engagement

Community Development

We work hand in hand with local communities to improve living conditions, strengthen livelihoods, and encourage active citizen participation through sustainable development initiatives.

  • Water and Sanitation Projects
  • Livelihood Improvement Programs
  • Volunteer Engagement
  • Community Mobilization
About Us

TIMOSA

Time of Sacrifice (TIMOSA) Environmental and Social Foundation has existed informally for four years. It is a community-based environmental and social development organization that promotes environmental protection, climate resilience, social welfare and sustainable livelihoods.
We partner with governments, organizations, and communities to deliver innovative environmental and social development projects.

  • Community Empowerment
  • Environmental Protection
  • Youth Development
