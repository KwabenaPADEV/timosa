About TIMOSA

Sacrificing Today For A Sustainable Tomorrow

Time of Sacrifice (TIMOSA) Environmental and Social Foundation is a community-based environmental and social development organization dedicated to protecting natural ecosystems, promoting climate resilience, and improving the quality of life of vulnerable communities.

The Foundation focuses on environmental conservation, social inclusion, sustainable livelihoods, public health, research, advocacy, and community empowerment through education, partnerships, and practical development initiatives.

TIMOSA believes that sustainable development can only be achieved when environmental protection and social progress work together. We therefore work with communities, stakeholders, and partners to create solutions that protect the planet while improving human wellbeing.

Our Vision

To become a leading environmental and social development organization that inspires sustainable communities, protects natural ecosystems, and improves the quality of life for present and future generations.

Our Mission

To promote environmental conservation, climate resilience, social inclusion, public health, and community empowerment through education, advocacy, research, partnerships, and sustainable development initiatives.

Our Strategic Position

Protecting Nature. Empowering Communities.

Building a sustainable future through environmental action, social responsibility, and community participation.

Time of Sacrifice (TIMOSA) Environmental and Social Foundation operates at the intersection of environmental protection and social development. We believe that communities can only thrive when people, nature, and sustainable systems work together.

Our approach combines environmental conservation, climate action, education, research, advocacy, and community-based solutions to address challenges affecting vulnerable communities.

Through partnerships, volunteerism, and stakeholder engagement, TIMOSA works towards creating environmentally responsible and socially inclusive communities capable of adapting to future challenges.

Strategic Objectives 2027 - 2031

Creating Measurable Impact Through Action

Our five-year strategic plan focuses on practical interventions that improve communities, protect nature, and promote sustainable development.

01

Environmental Education

Conduct environmental awareness campaigns in at least 50 schools and 40 communities across Ghana, reaching 20,000 people with knowledge on conservation, climate change, sanitation, and sustainable practices.

02

Tree Planting & Restoration

Plant and maintain 100,000 trees in degraded communities, schools, and public spaces while achieving a minimum 80% survival rate.

03

Waste Management

Establish waste segregation and recycling initiatives in at least 30 communities and educational institutions to reduce improper waste disposal.

04

Community Sanitation

Organize regular community clean-up exercises and environmental sanitation campaigns in communities across Ghana.

05

Climate Action

Train 5,000 youth and community leaders in climate adaptation, environmental sustainability, and disaster risk reduction.

06

Social Welfare & Empowerment

Provide education support, skills training, mentorship, and livelihood assistance to vulnerable youth, women, and disadvantaged groups.

07

Research & Publications

Produce research studies, policy briefs, and technical reports addressing environmental and social issues.

08

Partnerships & Resource Mobilization

Build strategic partnerships with government agencies, development organizations, academic institutions, and private sector organizations.

09

Volunteer Development

Recruit, train, and maintain a network of 500 active volunteers supporting environmental and social programmes nationwide.

10

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

Implement sustainable WASH projects in underserved communities to improve access to safe water, sanitation facilities, and hygiene education.
